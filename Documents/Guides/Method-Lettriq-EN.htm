Preambule

Tradition teaches that each of the letters comes from a primordial point called Yod, a grapheme corresponding to a particle of vibrating and moving energy in space which thus forms the substance of each of the letters of Hebrew creation.

The shape of the letters is not a symbol but a structuring function which organizes the animated matter of human life. The number of letters thus corresponds to the number of non-sexual chromosomes. The human is indeed in the beginning created male and female thanks to the meiosis and the fusion of the gametes. These consonant letters mathematically and geometrically define everything that exists, that has existed and that will exist, from the first to the last of the species.

The vowels, the melodic song which modulates the manifestation of speech, were initially not inscribed in the Sefer Torah: they are indeed of minor importance and they have been added in case the oral tradition is lost. The vowels were thus added with the Massorah. It seems that before this time, the Alef was pronounced "a", the He "o", the Vav "ou", the Yod "i", and one added "e, é, è" to a consonant for the breath of breathing. The first verse of the Bible can thus be read "bérashit béra aloim at oshémim ouat oarèts", HaShem hvhy "ioouo" and Torah hrvt "touro".

In reality, it does not matter which vowels are used, because it is the consonants and the Hebrew root which are important and which define the meaning of the words and verses of the Doctrine. Vowels are only conventions left for free will to the creature, because without structure there is no function. So even reading the Torah with a single vowel would be fine as long as the letters are not changed. Placing this or that vowel or using such a letter for such vowel is thus a matter of convenience of pronunciation and musical harmony. We can note, however, that the nature of sound is to permeate brain matter and to model the activity of non-coding genes, and that this sound is transmitted to the offspring in the same way as joy and stress.

Maimonides said "The Torah was given to achieve peace".

Lettriq

Programmed knowledge

The Hebrew alphabet is like the reference of the mnemonic instruction codes of the assembly chromosome programming and it allows the lettiq study of the key words of the oriented object language of knowledge exposed in the Torah.

The Hebrew Bible is not a literary text in spite of its apparent poetry: there is no “pun intended” relating to mocking humor, no “figure of style” relating to deception and no novelism relating to a story. temporal and historical: it is a software code relating the causally effective phenomena of the laws governing the universe and the human being.

Method

Ungrouping Items

The first step in the intuitive method to study a Hebrew word consists of studing the meaning of each letter and the common sense given by the dictionary and oral tradition.

For example, "Tov" which means "good, be good, pleasant, beautiful" is made up of the letters Tet, Vav and Bet.

The structural action of the Tet is to be the protection of the shield, its functional principle is the aesthetics of beauty and its numerical value is 9. Its intuitive sense is the refuge of the small boat that protects the traveler during his interiority .

Vav is the associativity of the nail and the interaction of communication, the ray of spiritual light, the spirit-matter connection, the mediation and the righteousness, the course and direction of business and travel. Vav is the couple's child as a daughter, just as Zayin is their son. These are the children of the Yod which correspond to the male and female gametes each containing one half of the potential of the spark of the consciousness of life (the spermatozoid Yod and the ovum Samek).

Bet is the house, the place of activities of personal, social and professional life, that is, the bearer of the flame of life, the abode of energy, the will, the temple and the solitary home. Bet is the man without the woman whom he meets with the He while walking towards the Torah to form his child becoming the 'Het then the Tet and so on, from the potential Alef to the realized Tav.

Tet + Vav + Bet = 9 + 6 + 2 = 17 or Yod-Zayin, the germ of the son.

Tet + Vav + Bet = 17 = 1 + 7 = 8 or 'Het, the creature.

We thus understand that the creature animated by the breathing of the breath in its principle of existence is a good thing as mentioned in Béréshit 1.21 and 1.25 "He will have taken care that [that {the existence of the animals} [is] good" , while it is in 3.6 it is mentioned "She took care, the Ishah,  of that the tree [of the knowledge of good and evil] be good for food [of Adam {gift of the Torah}]", because of the faculty of free will with which the human race is endowed and which gives it reason and understanding.

The Alef is a forward slash with two Yods. The Yod in the lower left is the woman who bears and supports the Yod in the upper right who is the man. The bar is the unique vision of the direction of the couple. The Alef is the archetype of the couple formed and turned towards the future. They are the ideal potential couple. From Bet to Shin, it is the process of formation and life of the couple. The Tav is the Resh (the stable man) of the Bet (the man in search) which is supported by the Yod of the woman with force: it is the result, the bar of the potential Alef merged with each one, the man and the woman are one flesh and have become the Tav.

Association of ideas

The second step consists in assembling the dissociated information according to the linguistic and narrative context of the text. It is generally a question of selecting for each letter a few conceptual words representing its meaning. The fundamental meaning of a word is thus the conceptual universe of all possible combinatorial associations. For example "Tov" means "refuge of the light of the will".

Conceptual correspondence

The third step is to assign an expression summarizing the meaning. It can be a concept, a real or imaginary thing, an environmental element or an organic, bodily or psychological structure.

"Tov" is good, it is that which is right, that which emanates goodness, that is to say the thoughts and actions which bring healthy and desirable things which do no harm.

According to the dictionary, Tov means: return, give back, respond. It is to answer for what happens and to be responsible for dealing with something, whether one is cause or effect, guilty or innocent.

Tov is made up of Tet-Vav (resistance, strength) associated withBet (house).

Tov is the resistance to the evil, and such is the peculiarity of the house of Israel.

Good is therefore the responsibility in the sense of having responsibility of something and of someone, in the singular as in the plural - not to be confused with being an involuntary or guilty author of a bad thing.
